Demon Valvetronic Technology

Our innovative Demon Valvetronic Technology brings about unprecedented versatility to the car owners. A simple key fob, a press of a button, you decide when and how you want to be heard. We are giving the power back to the driver, one to rule them all.

Demon app is now available for both Andoid and Ios. New generation control system allows users to cotrol their exhaust by using our lattest appplication with few simple clicks. The application has three similar option which is on, off and auto which are the same option available on our key fob. When on which is sport mode the valve will open to recieve more power and sound.When off which is the silent mode , the valve off to be quiet. Demon intelligent mode which is the auto option will turn on the valve above certain amount of RPM and turn off the valve bellow certain amount of RPM. The application also includes diagnostic tools for users to check the error code of ur car at ease.
